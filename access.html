2. Grant access to your Facebook Page:

Page Settings
We need Advertiser or Admin role on the Page. This lets us boost posts and run Page-Like campaigns.

3. Grant access to your Ad Account:

Ad Account Settings
We require Manage Campaigns permission. We never request billing permissions.

Events Manager

5. Instagram Business Account (if connected to BM):

Instagram Accounts

Website / Landing Page Access

We need access to your website or landing page platform to install tracking pixels (Meta, Google) and optimize conversion paths.

How to provide access: Add our team email akram@mobekmedia.site as an administrator or editor on your platform (WordPress, Shopify, Webflow, Unbounce, Wix, etc.). Alternatively, share a temporary login via a secure password manager (we recommend 1Password).

We need admin or developer access to insert Meta Pixel, Conversions API, and custom audiences. Don't worry — we only touch tracking scripts and never modify content/pricing.
